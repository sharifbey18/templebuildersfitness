Who we are?

Temple Builders Fitness is a premier fitness training company located in Atlanta, GA. We are a company that believes not only in fitness, but also in wholeness, and we are happy to offer our services to you. Not only do we want to see you in your best physical shape, but we want to see you thrive nutritionally and in the way you think about wellness.

Kokayi also works with the Boys and Girls Club as a fitness and sports coach and also helps local Cub Scouts fulfill fitness requirements for their fitness badge, too, while at the same time teaching that fitness can be fun.

Build your Temple Today

Kokayi Sutherland is a certified and insured personal trainer with over 15 years of experience. He is certified through the Biomechanical Research and Nutrition Institute (BRNI) and holds a Master Trainer certification through ExpertRating . He has extensive knowledge in weight training, strength and conditioning and High Impact Training (HIT) and is also EMS certified. Kokayi has developed successful workout regimens for children, teens, adults and seniors with weight management, muscle gain and rehabilitation therapy. Because he believes a significant part of healthy lifestyle change is proper nutrition, Kokayi also advices on nutritional supplementation and creates customized eating schedules. He is best known for creating fun, challenging workout routines without the use of traditional fitness equipment.
